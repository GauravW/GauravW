Hi!

I am Gaurav Waratkar, a first year PhD student at the Department of Physics, IIT Bombay.

.

With my guide Prof. Varun Bhalerao, I am studying high energy transients like Gamma-ray bursts and also building Daksha, a highly sensitive X-ray space telescope, proposed to be launched in 2023.

.

After spending 18 wonderful years of my life there, I moved to Mumbai for my Bachelors' in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Bombay. I moved my focus from Engineering to pursue a career in Astrophysics by joining Prof. Varun Bhalerao as a Graduate student starting in Jan 2020.
